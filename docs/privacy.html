Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

MOB Dashboard ("we," "our," or "the Application") is a business operations management tool that integrates with Google Workspace services to provide employee attendance monitoring, automated reporting, and email notifications.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Application. We are committed to safeguarding your data and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Access

MOB Dashboard accesses the following types of information through Google APIs:

Google Sheets Data

We read and write to Google Sheets that you explicitly configure for use with the Application. This includes employee attendance logs, operational data, and weekly report archives. We only access spreadsheets that you designate.

Gmail (Send Only)

We use the Gmail API exclusively to send automated email notifications on your behalf. We do not read, scan, index, or store the contents of your inbox, drafts, or any other Gmail data.

Google Drive (Read Only)

We access Google Drive in a read-only capacity to locate Google Sheets files by name. We do not modify, delete, or download files from your Drive.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data accessed through Google APIs is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Attendance Tracking: Recording employee sign-in/out events to designated Google Sheets.
  • Report Generation: Creating weekly summary reports based on operational data stored in your Sheets.
  • Email Notifications: Sending automated report summaries to email addresses you configure.
  • File Lookup: Locating configured Google Sheets within your Drive by filename.

4. Data Storage & Retention

MOB Dashboard operates as a locally-run application. We do not transmit your data to external servers or third-party services beyond Google's own APIs.

  • All operational data is stored in your Google Sheets within your Google Workspace.
  • OAuth tokens are stored locally on the machine where the Application is installed.
  • We do not maintain any external databases containing your personal or business data.
  • No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties. Data accessed through Google APIs is used exclusively for the purposes described in this policy and is never transferred to external services.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

MOB Dashboard's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only use Google user data for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties, except as necessary to provide the Application's functionality, comply with applicable laws, or as part of a merger/acquisition with adequate privacy protections.
  • We do not use Google user data to serve advertisements.
  • We do not allow humans to read Google user data unless we have your explicit consent, it is necessary for security purposes, to comply with laws, or the data is aggregated and anonymized for internal operations.

7. Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • OAuth 2.0 is used for secure authentication — we never handle your Google password.
  • API access tokens are stored securely on the local machine and are never transmitted to external servers.
  • The Application requests only the minimum permissions (scopes) necessary for its functionality.
  • All communication with Google APIs occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections.

8. Your Rights

You may revoke MOB Dashboard's access to your Google account at any time by visiting your Google Account Permissions page. Upon revocation:

  • The Application will immediately lose access to your Google Workspace data.
  • Locally stored OAuth tokens will become invalid.
  • Data already written to your Google Sheets will remain in your Sheets (under your control).

9. Children's Privacy

MOB Dashboard is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:
