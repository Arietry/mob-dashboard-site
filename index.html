Core Capabilities

Everything you need to manage operations

MOB Dashboard integrates with Google Workspace to automate your daily workflows.

Attendance Monitoring

Automatically tracks employee sign-in and sign-out activity, logs work sessions, and records daily attendance data in Google Sheets with real-time accuracy.

Automated Weekly Reports

Generates comprehensive weekly summary reports covering completed tasks, performance metrics, and operational highlights — delivered automatically to your team.

Email Notifications

Sends professionally formatted HTML email notifications via the Gmail API, including weekly report summaries with direct links to relevant Google Sheets.

Google Sheets Integration

Reads and writes data directly to Google Sheets for attendance logs, operational data, and report archives — keeping everything organized in your Workspace.

Workflow Automation

Orchestrates multiple services with sequential start-up, heartbeat monitoring, and quiet-mode scheduling — a true set-and-forget operations system.

Secure by Design

Uses Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication with minimal permission scopes. Your data stays in your own Google Workspace — we never store it on external servers.

How It Works

Simple, secure, automated

MOB Dashboard connects to your Google Workspace with read-only and minimal-write permissions.

01

Authorize Access

Sign in with your Google account and grant MOB Dashboard permission to access your Google Sheets and Gmail.

02

Configure Services

Set up your attendance monitoring targets, report schedules, and notification recipients through the dashboard.

03

Automate & Monitor

Let the system run. Attendance is tracked, reports are generated, and notifications are sent — all automatically.

Data & Permissions

Google API Scopes Used

MOB Dashboard requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function. Here's exactly what we access and why.

📊

Google Sheets API

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets

Used to read and write attendance logs, operational data, and weekly report archives to your Google Sheets.

✉️

Gmail Send

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send

Used exclusively to send automated weekly report email notifications to configured recipients. We do not read or access your inbox.

📁

Google Drive API (Read-only)

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly

Used to locate and reference Google Sheets files by name. No files are modified or deleted through this scope.
